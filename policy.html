Our Policies & User Trust Framework

UpCycleX is built on transparency, fairness, and community trust. This page explains how returns work, how ratings and rewards are calculated, and how we keep your experience safe, reliable, and rewarding.

↺ Return & Refund Policy

UpCycleX deals with upcycled and pre-loved products. To keep it fair for both creators and buyers, we follow a clear and time-bound return process.

  • Returns are accepted within 3–7 days of delivery, only if the product is significantly different from its listing (damage, wrong item, missing parts).
  • Users must upload clear photos/videos of the issue through the “Report an Issue” option in their order.
  • Our support team reviews the case and may contact both buyer and seller for clarification.
  • If approved, refunds are processed as: wallet credit or original payment mode, depending on availability.
  • Products marked as “Final Upcycle” or “No Return” are not eligible for return, and this is clearly shown on their listing page.

Note: Hygiene-sensitive, custom, or heavily modified items may not be returnable. Please review the listing details before purchase.

★ Ratings, Reviews & Stars

Ratings help the community discover trustworthy creators and buyers. We encourage honest, respectful, and experience-based feedback.

  • After each transaction, both buyer and seller can rate each other on a 1–5 star scale.
  • Users can add written reviews including photos of the received product to help others make better decisions.
  • Abusive, hateful, spam, or misleading reviews may be removed and may impact your account standing.
  • Repeated cancellations, no-shows, or violation of policies can lower your average rating and restrict some features.
  • We may highlight “Top Rated” creators and “Trusted Buyers” based on ratings and activity.

Your reviews should be based on actual experience and not used for harassment, blackmail, or unfair demands.

✦ Stars, Badges & Transformation Rewards

We celebrate every meaningful transformation of a product. Stars and badges are your proof of impact on the environment and the community.

  • For every successful product transformation (upload → accepted → sold or donated), creators earn impact stars.
  • Buyers who frequently choose upcycled items earn green stars.
  • Certain milestones unlock badges, such as:
    • Eco Starter • Upcycle Pro • Zero-Waste Champion • Community Mentor
  • Badges may unlock early access to new features, special events, or highlighted listing placement.
  • Misuse of the platform (fake listings, fraud, policy violations) can lead to removal of stars, badges, and restrictions.

Stars and badges are currently virtual rewards; any future monetary or redeemable value will be clearly mentioned in-app.

% Discount Vouchers & Incentives

Your positive actions on UpCycleX can unlock discount vouchers and special offers. We want to reward sustainable choices, not just purchases.

  • Earn vouchers by: completing transformations, maintaining high ratings, resolving disputes fairly, verifying your profile, and participating in community drives.
  • Vouchers can be applied during checkout on eligible products and may have minimum order values or validity dates.
  • Some vouchers are personal (non-transferable) and linked to your user ID for security.
  • Misuse (multiple fake accounts, self-transactions, coupon abuse) can lead to voucher cancellation.
  • Specific voucher terms (percentage, max discount, expiry) are shown clearly in your “Rewards / Vouchers” section.

UpCycleX may change or withdraw promotional campaigns from time to time, but active vouchers will honor their printed terms.

How We Ensure Trust & Safety

Trust is at the heart of UpCycleX. We combine transparent policies, community moderation, and secure technology to protect our users.

Verified profiles

Users can verify their email, phone, and (where available) ID. Verified users receive a trust badge on their profile and listings.

Secure payments

Payments are processed through trusted gateways. Sensitive data like card details is handled by certified partners, not stored directly by us.

Transparent history

You can view ratings, reviews, and transaction history indicators for both buyers and sellers before you interact with them.

Moderation & reporting

Any suspicious activity or content can be reported directly from the app. Our team reviews reports and may warn, suspend, or ban violators.

For urgent safety concerns, please stop all communication, avoid payments outside the platform, and use the in-app “Report” feature or contact our support team immediately.
